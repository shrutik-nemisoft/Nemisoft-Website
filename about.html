About nemisoft

We build ERP that works
for you, not against you.

A technical ERP consultancy headquartered in India, serving businesses across the Middle East, UK, and South Asia. Certified NetSuite developer. Official Odoo partner.

Our story

Built by engineers,
for growing businesses.

nemisoft was founded with one conviction: ERP software should reduce complexity, not create it. Most businesses come to us after years of fighting a system that was never truly configured for how they work.

We come in, listen to how your business actually operates, and build around that — not the other way around. Whether it's a full NetSuite implementation, an Odoo deployment, a complex API integration, or an AWS migration, we bring the technical depth to get it right the first time.

Eight years and 50+ clients later, we have learned that the best ERP project is one you never have to revisit because something broke.

Certified & Partnered

Certified NetSuite Developer and official Odoo Partner — not resellers, but deeply technical implementers who build what the documentation doesn't cover.

Global Reach, Local Context

Based in India, with active projects in UAE, UK, and South Asia. We understand the compliance, tax, and operational nuances of each market.

No Black Boxes

Every implementation is documented, tested, and handed over so your team can maintain it — no lock-in, no dependency on us for day-to-day operations.

AWS-Backed Infrastructure

As AWS Solution Architects, we don't just implement ERP — we ensure the infrastructure it runs on is resilient, scalable, and cost-efficient.
