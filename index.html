NetSuite

You picked the right ERP. We help you get everything out of it.

Choosing NetSuite already put you ahead. Our job is to make it fit your business — and take the system-level headaches off your plate so you can focus on the work that matters.

/devCustom Development

Scripts, workflows, and customizations built on NetSuite best practices — designed to be configurable, so your team can make small changes without calling us back.

/adminManaged Administration

Users, roles, subsidiaries, dashboards, saved searches, and release support. We run the admin layer so it stays clean, controlled, and audit-ready.

/supportSupport & Maintenance

Day-to-day operational support, troubleshooting, and engagement with NetSuite for defect resolution — without your operations grinding to a halt.

/trainTraining

Hands-on training from certified professionals that builds real confidence, so the transition to a new system stops feeling like a hurdle.

Odoo

One platform for your whole business. We make it fit perfectly.

As an official Odoo partner, we implement, extend, and support Odoo across every module your business relies on — and connect it to the rest of your stack.

/implImplementation

End-to-end Odoo rollouts — from requirements scoping and data migration to go-live and hypercare. We configure every module to match how your team actually works.

/devCustom Modules

When standard Odoo doesn't cover your edge case, we build it. Custom modules developed to Odoo standards so upgrades don't break your business logic.

/migrateMigration

Moving off a legacy ERP or an older Odoo version? We handle the data mapping, cleansing, and cutover — with minimal disruption to day-to-day operations.

/supportSupport & Training

Ongoing functional and technical support, plus hands-on training so your team can operate Odoo confidently without depending on us for every task.
